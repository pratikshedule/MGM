About Us

We are the world's largest university

MGM University, officially known as Maharashtra Global Marketing University, is a distinguished institution dedicated to providing quality education and fostering research across various disciplines. Located in Aurangabad, Maharashtra, it offers a diverse range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs, with a strong emphasis on innovation and practical learning. The university is known for its state-of-the-art infrastructure, well-equipped laboratories, and a robust library, which support an enriching academic environment. With a focus on holistic development, MGM University promotes extracurricular activities, encouraging students to engage in sports, cultural events, and community service. The faculty comprises experienced educators and industry professionals, ensuring that students receive a comprehensive education that prepares them for the challenges of the modern workforce. Additionally, the university fosters collaborations with various industries and academic institutions, enhancing research opportunities and facilitating internships for students. With a commitment to excellence and a vision to empower the youth, MGM University plays a pivotal role in shaping the future of its students and contributing to the socio-economic development of the region.
